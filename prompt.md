2. Garden — The Main Visualization
The emotional heart of the product. Your recent days as a living garden.

A horizontal scrollable garden bed. Each plant is a day, and below each plant is the day of the week and date.
Plants vary in species based on what kind of day it was — a social day might be a flower, a solo focused day a tall mushroom, a scattered day a small mossy cluster.
Height/Glow intensity is the density of the day. Really memorable days have a higher glow intensity.
Today's plant is marked with some kind of marker, still growing. Maybe it's bigger and in the center?
Tap any plant to open that day's detail — waveform, events, what drove the density, option to reflect (tend).
A "tend" action on past plants — add a note, attach a photo, write a memory. This brightens the plant retroactively, simulating the neuroscience of memory reconsolidation (revisiting a memory strengthens it).
For future events, you are able to see whatever is planned as well as suggestions for how to make that day more memorable.
